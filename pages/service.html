Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh are your products?
All our products are sourced daily from local farms and suppliers. We guarantee maximum freshness and quality for every item delivered to your doorstep.
Do you deliver outside Phnom Penh?
Yes! We offer same-day delivery within Phnom Penh and nationwide shipping to all provinces in Cambodia. Delivery times may vary based on location.
What if I'm not satisfied with my order?
We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange.
Are your products organic?
We offer both organic and conventionally grown products. All items are clearly labeled so you can make informed choices based on your preferences.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can use this to track your delivery in real-time through our website or app.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, ABA Pay, Wing, and all major mobile banking apps in Cambodia.
